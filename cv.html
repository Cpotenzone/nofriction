Executive Profile

No friction started as a small consulting entity in Estonia, founded by Casey Potenzone in 2018. It has over the years operated subs in Berlin and The Hague and done business across the world from Dubai to Paris. As Casey and his family relocated (back) to California, the focus of his business and no friction shifted.

With a proven commercial track record of transforming ideas into market-ready products, and turning around companies, he has led projects for industry giants like Apple and MTV and emerging startups like uniloc and aiDaptive.

His expertise in modern IP monetization and digital-first strategies helped Nexway Group achieve $250 million a year in turnover, and arvato just over $100 million in ecommerce. He helped nobilia launch their KutchenHaus ecom site, generating record revenue when most others were failing.
