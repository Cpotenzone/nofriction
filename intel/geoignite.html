Technical Approach

Syntin's Distillation Process

Phase 1: Data Preparation

CriticalAsset.com's proprietary data is organized and structured with primary keys for assets, time series measurements, and geospatial references. This creates the foundation for the knowledge garden that will power GeoIgnite.

Phase 2: Teacher Model Training

Syntin develops a comprehensive "teacher" model using the full depth and breadth of CriticalAsset.com's data repository. This large-scale model captures complex relationships between assets, their performance over time, and their spatial context.

Phase 3: Knowledge Distillation

Through an iterative process, the knowledge from the teacher model is compressed into a much smaller "student" model. This distillation preserves critical decision-making capabilities while dramatically reducing computational requirements.

Phase 4: Optimization & Refinement

The distilled model undergoes rigorous testing and optimization to ensure it maintains accuracy while operating within constrained computing environments. Fine-tuning is performed against real-world scenarios to validate performance.
