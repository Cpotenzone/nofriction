PREVIEW: INSIDE THE SUBSIDY TRAP

OpenAI: A Case Study in Unsustainable Economics

OpenAI presents the most striking example of the economic disconnect:

  • $13 billion on compute with Microsoft alone in 2025
  • $12.9 billion five-year compute deal with CoreWeave (~$2.38 billion annually)
  • $19 billion commitment to the Stargate data center project
  • Total 2025 costs: $28 billion+ with projected revenue of $11 billion
  • Projected 2025 losses: $14+ billion while growing its customer base

The economic fundamentals of this approach are clearly unsustainable...
