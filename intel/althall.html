AMPLIFYING OPPORTUNITY, CREATING LEGACY

"Leaving alternative artists and alternative music community better than we found them."

The Alternative Music Hall of Fame (Alt-Hall) is dedicated to honoring legends of alternative music while creating transformative opportunities for emerging artists and the wider music community.

Through our flagship initiative, the "A Place for Everyone" Young Musicians Resource Center, Alt-Hall provides comprehensive programming designed to create meaningful connections between industry veterans and emerging artists.

WE NEED YOUR SUPPORT

We are seeking donated resources to fund our programs – not our physical building – but the vital administrative costs, program development, and community outreach initiatives that make our mission possible.

YOUR DONATIONS FUND THESE PROGRAMS

Every dollar contributed directly supports our "A Place for Everyone" Young Musicians Resource Center and its seven core programs

Instruments and Equipment Acquisition

Providing access to quality instruments and equipment for young musicians who may not have the financial means to purchase their own.

Rehearsal Spaces and Studio Resources

Creating partnerships with studios and venues to provide affordable or free rehearsal and recording spaces for developing artists.

Recording Workshops & Engineer Mentoring

Offering hands-on training with industry professionals to develop technical skills in recording, mixing, and production.

Legal/Contractual Advocacy

Providing guidance on management, touring, streaming, and label contracts to help artists protect their interests and rights.

Industry Veteran Summits & Mentoring

Facilitating meaningful mentoring relationships between established artists and newcomers to share knowledge and experience.

Recording Equipment and Software Workshops

Training the next generation of recording professionals through hands-on workshops with industry-standard tools.

Mental Wellness for Musicians

Addressing the unique mental health challenges that musicians face with specialized resources, workshops, and support groups led by professionals.

MEASURABLE IMPACT

Our programs are designed to achieve these measurable outcomes in our community:

Decreased Social Isolation

Building meaningful connections in the music community

Increased Social Wellness

Fostering a supportive environment for creative growth

Enhanced Support Networks

Creating stronger safety nets for at-risk artists

Reduced "Bunkerism"

Decreasing over-indulgence in virtual reality among youth

Diverse Music Appreciation

Expanding awareness of various musical styles and performances

Cross-Cultural Awareness

Building bridges between different cultural expressions

Intergenerational Understanding

Fostering respect and knowledge sharing between music veterans and emerging artists
