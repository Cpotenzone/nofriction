Key Insights & Recommendations

Strategic opportunities to improve user engagement and product experience

High-Value User Focus

Amteck users dominate activity, particularly with lighting assets (Strip Light T4F, Strip Light T2F). Consider developing deeper integrations and premium features for lighting components to enhance value for these power users.

Feature Development Priority

The Asset page receives over 64% of all activity. Prioritize enhancements to asset management workflows, improved bulk editing capabilities, and advanced filtering options to increase efficiency.

Engagement Opportunity

User activity peaks midweek during business hours (10am-2pm). Schedule product announcements, webinars and support resources during these peak engagement times to maximize reach and impact.

Retention Strategy

Implement targeted onboarding improvements for companies with lower engagement patterns. Consider dedicated account management for Nintendo and CSU users who demonstrate high retention and consistent engagement.

Growth Opportunity

Creation events (36%) and updating assets (52%) dominate user activities. Consider adding collaborative features like commenting, sharing, and version control to enhance team workflows and drive deeper platform adoption.

Product Development

The high frequency of Asset Plan page usage suggests users need better ways to visualize relationships. Develop enhanced visualization tools and reporting capabilities to better serve this need.

Executive Summary

The user data reveals a platform with strong engagement from technical users primarily focused on asset management and planning. Amteck emerges as the most active company with significant activity around lighting components. Users demonstrate consistent engagement patterns with high retention rates among Nintendo and CSU users.

To drive growth, focus product development on enhancing asset management capabilities, adding collaboration features, and improving visualization tools. Target marketing efforts toward electrical engineering firms with similar profiles to your top users, and consider developing industry-specific templates to accelerate onboarding.
