Method and apparatus for detection of tampering attacks

Filed: 2005

Granted: USPTO

Inventors: Casey Potenzone et al.

A method for detecting an attempted attack on a security system. In one preferred embodiment, the method includes retrieving a parameter from a hardware system that changes during normal operation, comparing with previously stored parameters, and detecting lack of change indicating tampering.
