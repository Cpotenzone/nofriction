Insider Planning Tips

Pro tips from locals to make your Santa Cruz family adventure unforgettable

Best Weather

September-October offers warmest days with less fog. Pack layers year-round as coastal weather changes quickly.

Timing Tips

Arrive at fire ring beaches by 10am to secure spots. Weekdays are less crowded for most attractions.

Money Saving

Many state parks allow free entry if you park outside gates. Kids fish free at all piers without licenses.
