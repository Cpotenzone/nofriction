Special Note: Fish stocking temporarily suspended - check status

Summer Fishing Pro Tips

Best Times: Dawn (5:30-8:30am) and dusk (6:30-8:30pm)

Avoid: 11am-4pm (too hot, fish inactive)

Deep Water: Target thermocline 25-35ft in lakes

Summer Gear: Topwater early, deep jigs midday

Water Temp: Monitor for 70-75°F sweet spots

Stream Status: Most closed until winter rains
