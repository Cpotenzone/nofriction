2️⃣ Data Narrative Framework

SYNTIN organizes all enterprise knowledge into five narrative categories, each representing a critical dimension of business intelligence.

Strategy Narrative

All information related to strategic planning and execution.

Data Scope Strategic planning documents, OKRs, competitive intelligence, M&A plans, board presentations
Ownership C-suite leadership, Chief Strategy Officer
Key Metrics Goal attainment %, strategic project ROI, market share targets

Leadership Narrative

Information about leadership effectiveness and organizational structure.

Data Scope Leadership assessments, succession plans, organizational structure, management communications
Ownership HR executives, C-suite leadership
Key Metrics Leadership diversity indices, manager engagement scores, executive tenure

Culture Narrative

All data reflecting organizational culture and employee experience.

Data Scope Employee engagement surveys, eNPS scores, retention statistics, DEI metrics
Ownership HR/People team, COO, department heads
Key Metrics Employee satisfaction, retention rates, cultural health indicators

Value Narrative

Data on business outcomes and value creation.

Data Scope Revenue, profit, EBITDA, cash flow, productivity measures, efficiency ratios
Ownership CFO, COO, business unit heads, FP&A teams
Key Metrics Financial performance, operational efficiency, customer value metrics

Brand Narrative

External perception and market-facing metrics.

Data Scope Brand awareness, NPS, social sentiment, share of voice, PR coverage, customer loyalty
Ownership CMO, marketing/brand management team
Key Metrics Brand perception, customer satisfaction, market reputation
