Hair Care & Hormonal Health

⚠️ Chemical Hair Products & Metabolism:

Research shows hair relaxers contain endocrine disruptors that can affect hormone balance, insulin sensitivity, and metabolism.

Safer Alternatives:

  • • Natural protective styles (braids, twists)
  • • Heat styling instead of chemical relaxers
  • • Organic/natural hair products
  • • Regular scalp detox treatments
  • • Minimize chemical exposure frequency

When to Adjust Your Plan

Reduce Calories If:

  • • No weight change for 2+ weeks
  • • Energy remains high
  • • Not feeling hungry between meals
  • • Performance isn't declining

Reduce by 100-150 calories

Increase Calories If:

  • • Losing weight too quickly (>2lbs/week)
  • • Energy consistently low
  • • Performance declining
  • • Mood or sleep disrupted

Increase by 100-200 calories

Take a Diet Break If:

  • • Plateaued for 3+ weeks
  • • Experiencing high stress
  • • Sleep quality poor
  • • Extreme hunger or cravings

Return to maintenance for 1-2 weeks
