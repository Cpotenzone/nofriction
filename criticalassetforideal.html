White Label Solution: "IDEAL Intelligence™"

A comprehensive suite of intelligent modules that transform IDEAL's hardware capabilities into a complete MEP intelligence ecosystem.

01 IDEAL Circuit Intelligence™

Uses data from SureTrace circuit tracers to create dynamic digital twins of electrical systems, enabling visualization and management of entire electrical infrastructures.

Key Features:

  • Interactive electrical panel visualizations
  • Circuit mapping and load analysis
  • Historical tracking of circuit changes
  • Anomaly detection in electrical systems

Integration:

Direct connection to IDEAL SureTrace and SureTrace Plus circuit tracers, with mobile app scanning capabilities for QR-coded panels and circuits.

02 IDEAL Thermal Intelligence™

Leverages infrared thermometer data for predictive maintenance, identifying potential failures before they occur through temperature trend analysis.

Key Features:

  • Thermal mapping of facilities
  • Historical temperature trend analysis
  • Predictive maintenance alerts
  • Integration with maintenance scheduling

Integration:

Compatible with IDEAL's infrared thermometers, allowing data upload through mobile app or Bluetooth connectivity.

03 IDEAL Power Intelligence™

Uses multimeter and clamp meter data to optimize energy usage, track power quality, and identify energy waste across facilities.

Key Features:

  • Power consumption analytics
  • Load balancing recommendations
  • Energy optimization suggestions
  • Power quality monitoring

Integration:

Works with IDEAL's TightSight clamp meters and digital multimeters through manual data entry or future IoT-enabled devices.

04 IDEAL Safety Intelligence™

Creates predictive safety insights from testing data, helping prevent accidents and ensuring compliance with safety regulations.

Key Features:

  • Safety compliance tracking
  • Risk assessment visualization
  • Incident prevention recommendations
  • Regulatory requirement monitoring

Integration:

Aggregates data from receptacle testers, voltage testers, and other safety equipment to create comprehensive safety profiles.
