How IDEAL Predict™ Would Work

The Technology Integration

  1. Data Collection: IDEAL's test & measurement tools (circuit tracers, multimeters, infrared thermometers) capture critical electrical and thermal data points.
  2. Secure Cloud Transmission: Data is securely transmitted to the IDEAL Predict™ platform (powered by CriticalAsset's SLM technology).
  3. Spatial-Temporal Analysis: Platform correlates measurements with location data and historical patterns to generate predictions.
  4. Actionable Intelligence: Electricians and facility managers receive predictive alerts, maintenance recommendations, and system optimization guidance.

Core Platform Capabilities

Predictive Failure Detection

Identify potential circuit breaker, wiring, and equipment failures before they occur based on electrical signature analysis and thermal patterns.

Maintenance Optimization

Prioritize maintenance activities based on predicted failure risk, optimizing resource allocation and preventing costly downtime.

Digital Twin Creation

Build comprehensive digital representations of electrical systems, mapping components spatially for advanced troubleshooting.

Energy Optimization

Identify energy waste and inefficiencies in electrical systems, recommending corrective actions that reduce consumption.

Historical Analytics

Track system performance over time, identifying trends and patterns that inform preventative maintenance strategies.

Compliance Documentation

Automatically generate documentation for electrical system inspections, maintenance, and testing for regulatory compliance.
